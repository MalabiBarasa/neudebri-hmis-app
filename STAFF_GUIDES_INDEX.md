## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Use temporary passwords** like "Temp@2024" for new staff
   - They must change on first login
   - More secure than knowing their real password

2. **Keep a staff register** with username, employee ID, department
   - Useful for tracking who has what access
   - Helpful when staff leaves

3. **Use unique employee IDs** like "DOC001", "NUR002"
   - Makes staff easy to identify
   - Useful for reports and audits

4. **Test new staff accounts** by logging in
   - Verify everything works before they start
   - Prevents first-day surprises

5. **Train staff on their first day**
   - Show them the dashboard
   - Explain their role-specific features
   - Practice with sample data

6. **Never delete user accounts**
   - Deactivate instead (unchecks "Active")
   - Preserves audit trail and history
   - Can reactivate later if needed
